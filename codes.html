What are Rerolls?

Rerolls allow you to re-randomize the stats on your crafted weapons and armor. Use them to get better multipliers and traits on your gear!

How to Redeem Codes

Not sure how to redeem codes in The Forge? Follow these simple steps:

1

Launch The Forge

Open Roblox and launch The Forge game. Wait for it to fully load.

2

Open Settings

Select the cog button in the top left corner of your screen to open the Settings menu.

3

Find the Codes Section

Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings menu to find the 'Codes' section.

4

Enter Your Code

Type or paste your code into the text box, then press the 'Claim' button to receive your reward!

Code Tips

Codes are case-sensitive! Make sure to enter them exactly as shown, including any capital letters or special characters like exclamation marks.
